Your First Day

Can You Survive a Day in Super School?

This choose your own adventure game lets you live a day in the life of a super student. Your decisions throguhout your first day at Super School will help you discover your powers, or lack thereof, make firends and determine your future place in the world of super heros. It all depends on your first day and the choices that you make to determine your place as either a hero, sidekick, super villain, Super School dropout or rogue. Let classes begin!
